Looking for an excuse to ride the ferry? Want to learn how to BBQ? Need an outlet to let your competitive edge shine? Brooklyn Tech Key Club and Stuyvesant Key Club bring you our annual Adventures at Governor’s Island Fundraiser. Join us on September 25th for BBQ-ing picnicking field games and inter school contests. 

All proceeds will be going to the Governor's Project, Soles4Souls  This nonprofit organization receives shoe and clothing donations from individuals, community donation drives, and retailers. Then, they distribute the donated items to various programs around the world to help those in need.
