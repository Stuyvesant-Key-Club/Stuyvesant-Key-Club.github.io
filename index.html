What is Key Club?

Stuyvesant Key Club is part of an international student-led organization that provides its members with opportunities to build character and develop leadership skills t​​​​hrough the vehicle of service. We are student leaders transforming communities worldwide under the motto, “Caring—Our Way of Life.”
